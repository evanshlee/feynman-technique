## What to Notice

- **One gap per turn** strictly enforced
- **Praise is specific**, not generic
- **Every AI turn ends in a question** - dialog, not lecture
- **Scaffolds are capped** and must hand the turn back to the learner
- **Mastery requires 4 of 5 criteria**, tracked across turns
- **Refined explanation preserves user phrasings** from the session

## Contributing Your Own Example

Run a real session, save the transcript, and PR it. Guidelines:

- **Different domain than existing examples** (history, mathematics, biology, design, law, etc.)
- **Full session** from kickoff to mastery
- **Include at least one edge case or stuck moment**
- **~100-200 lines** - long enough to be useful, short enough to read
- **Add your example to the table above**

Anonymize if sensitive. The goal is teaching the pattern, not showcasing your personal knowledge.
